Our Story

Founded in 2008, CREDAI began with a simple intention: “to bring trust back into real estate.” Today, with a strong network of over 190 member developers, that purpose remains unchanged.

Every member adheres to the CREDAI Code of Conduct, upholding ethical and transparent practices, while the Consumer Redressal Forum reinforces accountability by resolving homebuyer concerns. This consistent focus on credibility is what shaped our journey and continues to inspire confidence every day.
